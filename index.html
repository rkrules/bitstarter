LifeLog is an application where you can collect your memories - be it on a daily basis or just of a trip. You can choose to type or just voice record. You can add pictures or video. You can set reminders to input daily or just do it yourself whenever you wish to. The power of the app lies in its ability to share individual memories or stitch multiple inputs into a shareable format.

You can create many projects such as 1 pictures a day (reference), 1 second everyday, 3 awesome things everyday, Dream logs and so on. This app does not bore you to do death with multiple configuration items for regular use, at the same time app is highly customizable for an advanced user. Keep it completely private or share it on Social Media accounts - entirely your choice. Post on a blog with all multimedia or just print a pdf/paperback with text and pictures.
